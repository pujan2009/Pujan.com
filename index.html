Crop Production


Production is the process of growing crops for domestic and commercial purposes.

  • Preparation of Soil.
  • Sowing of Seeds.
  • Irrigation.
  • Application of manure, pesticides, and fertilizers to the crops.
  • Protecting and Harvesting Crops.
  • Storage and Preserving the produced Crops.
  1. Preparation of Soil

Prepraing the soil for sowing the seeds.

  • Ploughing
  • levelling
  • manuring
  1. Sowing of seeds

Sowing is the process by which seeds are buried inside the soil.

  • Traditional method
  • Mordern method
  1. Irrigating the Soil

Irrigation is the process of applying water to the crops artificially to fulfil their water requirements.

  • Surface
  • Sprinkler
  • Drip/trickle
  • Subsurface
  1. Harvesting of crops

Once the crop is matured or fully ripen, they are cut and gathered (Reaping) which are collectively called as harvesting.

  • Hand harvesting
  • Harvesting with hand tools
  • Harvesting with machinery.
  1. Storage of crops

On a small or medium scale, farmers store them in metal containers or jute bags.
